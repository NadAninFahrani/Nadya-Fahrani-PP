A reflection journal will help you discover more about yourself rather than a step-by-step blog. Everyone is different in their own ways. You should never feel bad if one method of coping doesn't work for you. Instead, dig deep within yourself and without any judgement, reflect.
The emotional bar at the bottom of each journal will represent your day. The smile representing the best emotional control and the frown representing poor emotional control.
